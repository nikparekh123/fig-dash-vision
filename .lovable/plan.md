

# Figma (FIG) Financial Dashboard — Dark & Modern

## Overview
A single-page, dark-themed financial dashboard showcasing Figma's Q4 2025 earnings data with interactive charts and key metrics.

## Layout

### 1. Header
- Company name "Figma (NYSE: FIG)" with a stock-style header
- Subtitle: "Q4 2025 Earnings Dashboard"

### 2. Key Metrics Row (KPI Cards)
Four highlight cards across the top:
- **Q4 Revenue**: $303.8M (+40% YoY) — green accent
- **Adjusted EPS**: $0.08 (beat $0.06 estimate) — green accent
- **GAAP Net Loss**: -$226.6M — red accent
- **FY2025 Revenue**: $1.06B (+41%) — green accent

### 3. Revenue Chart
- **Bar chart** comparing quarterly/annual revenue with YoY growth percentages
- Show Q4 2025 actual ($303.8M) alongside FY2025 ($1.06B) and FY2026 guidance midpoint (~$1.37B)

### 4. Profitability Chart
- **Combined bar + line chart** showing net income/loss and EBITDA margin
- FY2025 net loss of -$1.25B, EBITDA margin of -120.7%
- Projected FY2026 EPS of $0.25 shown as a forward-looking data point

### 5. Guidance Section
- Visual comparison cards for Q1 2026 guidance ($315–317M) vs. consensus
- FY2026 revenue forecast range ($1.366–1.374B) with a progress-style indicator
- Projected EPS of $0.25 highlighted as the path to profitability

### 6. Summary Section
- The narrative text from your summary, styled as a readable brief with section headers ("Revenue Roars", "Guidance: Bullish on 2026") and emoji accents

## Design
- **Dark background** with subtle card borders and glow effects
- **Green/red accent colors** for positive/negative metrics
- **Recharts** for all chart components
- Responsive layout that works on desktop and tablet
